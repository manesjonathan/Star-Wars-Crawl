A long time ago

I am originally from Mettet, Belgium. I grew up in a small town and always loved finding new ways to be creative. After finishing high school, I decided to study interior design. I moved to Brussels to study at the higher institute of interior design and graduated in 2012. After working as an interior designer for a few years, I started to feel like I needed a new challenge. That's when I decided to learn Java and start developing Android applications. In 2021, I released my first app, Athan Nemas, on the Google Play Store.
